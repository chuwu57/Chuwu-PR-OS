# Example: OPPO Mother's Day Copy

## Input

```text
我妈有两个“老公”，一个是我爸，另一个一年见两回。
跟我爸约会基本不打扮，见另一个，她恨不得穿婚纱。
```

## Likely Intent

The creators likely wanted to say that mothers are not only family caregivers; they can also have fandom, passion, and personal joy.

## Why It Is High Risk

The issue is not whether mothers can follow celebrities. The issue is the collision between:

- Mother's Day as a high-respect family occasion;
- "老公" as a strong public marriage term;
- fandom slang as an internal circle term;
- "婚纱" and "约会" as intimate/romantic imagery;
- a screenshotable line: "我妈有两个老公";
- a gender framing where the mother's passion is narrated through another male object.

## Example Conclusion

Score: 80-90 / 100.

Level: crisis risk.

Advice: do not release; preserve the strategic intent but rewrite the expression.

## Safer Rewrite Directions

Conservative safe:

```text
妈妈不只属于家庭、日程和责任。她也有自己的热爱、舞台和心动时刻。
```

Youthful but non-offensive:

```text
原来妈妈也会追星，也会尖叫，也会为了热爱认真打扮。
今天，不只祝她母亲节快乐，也祝她做自己快乐。
```

High-creative but low-misread:

```text
小时候，我以为妈妈只会为我奔赴。
后来才发现，她也会为自己的热爱奔赴千里。
```

# 热点舆情分析 / Hot Public Opinion Analysis

## 中文

这个 Skill 用来把外部热点转成业务判断。

适用场景：

- 热搜、行业争议、同业事故、政策变化、负面叙事、网帖传闻。
- 判断事件是否已经形成舆情。
- 判断对品牌信任、行业语境、业务风险和公关资产的影响。

默认输出：

- 是否已经形成舆情。
- 舆情实质分析。
- 舆情走势研判。
- 对业务的参考意义。

与判断中枢的关系：

本 Skill 负责判断事件性质和业务相关性；如果要决定是否回应、自查、做预案或调整动作，交给判断中枢收口。

## English

This skill turns external hot topics into business judgment.

Use cases:

- Trending topics, industry controversies, peer-company incidents, policy changes, negative narratives, and online rumors.
- Judge whether a topic has become public opinion.
- Assess impact on brand trust, industry context, business risk, and PR assets.

Default output:

- Whether public opinion has formed.
- Substance of the issue.
- Likely trajectory.
- Business implications.

Relationship with the judgment hub:

This skill diagnoses the issue and its relevance. The judgment hub decides whether to respond, self-check, prepare a contingency plan, or change business actions.
